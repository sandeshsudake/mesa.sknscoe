About MESA

The Mechanical Engineering Students' Association (MESA) is a dynamic student body dedicated to fostering excellence and innovation among mechanical engineering students. We provide a platform for students to enhance their technical skills, explore career opportunities, and engage in co-curricular activities.

  • ✔ Hands-on workshops and technical sessions
  • ✔ Expert talks and industrial visits
  • ✔ Design and innovation challenges
  • ✔ Networking opportunities with professionals
